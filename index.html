Frequently Asked Questions

How does delivery confirmation work?
Once we receive your booking form through the website, we will check our availability and text you immediately to let you know if we can deliver within the 1-hour window.
What payment methods are supported for Tap to Pay?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, and Google Pay using our secure portable tap reader terminal when we arrive at your house.
Where are you guys located?
We are based right here in Southwest Calgary. Because we travel on foot or by bike, we only deliver to Upper Mount Royal, Lower Mount Royal, Bankview, Marda Loop, and South Calgary.
Why are you running this business?
We are moving into Grade 8! We wanted to take initiative and raise $150 to buy our own binders, notebooks, pens, and school gear for the upcoming academic year. Thanks for supporting local kids!
